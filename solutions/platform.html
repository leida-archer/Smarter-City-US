The Platform

Three Products. One Integrated Ecosystem.

The only platform where permits, enforcement, and payments reinforce each other — sharing data in real time to eliminate silos.

Contact Us

How the pieces connect

Every product reinforces the others. Data flows in real time, not overnight batches.

vPermit

Defines who has the right to park, where, and when.

Validates rights

vCompliance

Enforces parking rules with LPR and real-time validation.

Confirms payment

vPark

Handles payments with differential rates tied to permits.

One data layer. Zero silos.

01

Permits Validate Enforcement

When an LPR camera reads a plate, vCompliance instantly checks vPermit data. Valid permit holders are never cited by mistake.

02

Payments Link to Permits

vPark knows if a parker has a permit and applies differential rates automatically. Permit holders may park free; visitors pay by the minute.

03

Enforcement Validates Rights

Active vPark sessions are visible to vCompliance in real time. Parkers with valid sessions are never cited.

04

Unified Reporting

One dashboard for permits, citations, and revenue. No reconciliation across separate systems.

05

Single Customer Record

Students, staff, and visitors have one profile across all three products. One login, one vehicle list, one payment method.

06

API-First Architecture

Every feature is accessible via API. Integrate with your student & employee information systems, payments, and BI tools without custom development.
