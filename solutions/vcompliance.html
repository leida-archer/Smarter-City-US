vCompliance

Enforcement & Citation Management

LPR-powered enforcement with real-time validation and automated workflows.

Request a Demo

Smarter enforcement, better outcomes

Modern tools that improve compliance, increase officer efficiency, and create a better experience for your community.

Advanced LPR Integration

Support for mobile and fixed LPR cameras from multiple vendors. Automatic plate reads validated against permit data in real time.

Electronic & Physical Citations

Issue citations electronically or via handheld devices. Full audit trail with photo evidence and GPS location.

Real-Time Validation

Instantly verify parking rights against permits, payments, and special permissions before issuing any citation.

Customer Portal

Online payment, appeals submission, and citation history. Reduce in-person visits and phone call volume.

Route Optimization

AI-assisted patrol routes that maximize coverage and minimize fuel costs for enforcement officers.

Automated Tasks & Communications

Customizable HTML communications for violations, escalations, and payment reminders. Automated task assignments.

How it works

01

Patrol & Scan

Officers patrol routes while LPR cameras automatically read and log license plates.

02

Validate Rights

Each plate is checked against all active permits, payments, and special permissions in real time.

03

Issue Citation

Violations are flagged for officer review. Citations issued electronically with photo evidence.

04

Customer Self-Service

Violators pay online, submit appeals, and view their history through the customer portal.
