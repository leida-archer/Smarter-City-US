7 ways to start a session

IVR, Web, Map, QR, Search, Direct Entry, and App — every parker gets the experience that suits them.

Unique to vPark

Single Sign-On with Your Campus Login

Parkers sign in with their existing campus or city credentials — Banner, Workday, Google, Microsoft, Okta. No new accounts, no new passwords.

PayGo & QR Code Payment

Scan a QR code on-site to start a session instantly. No app download required for first-time users.

Voice-Activated Parking (Alexa)

"Alexa, start my parking session." The first voice-activated parking payment system on the market.

Differential Rates & Real-Time Validation

Dynamic pricing based on permit status, time of day, zone, and occupancy. Rates adjust automatically.

Apple Pay / Google Pay / Any Payment

Accept every major payment method. Credit, debit, mobile wallets, and campus card systems.

Automated Zone Alerts & Availability

Real-time occupancy data, expiration reminders, and zone availability notifications for parkers.

Corporate Accounts & Pre/Post-Paid

Organizations can set up corporate accounts with pre-paid balances or post-paid invoicing.
