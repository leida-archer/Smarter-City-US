vPermit

Virtual Permit Management

Unlimited rules, SSO, and self-service for every parker — students, staff, visitors, and contractors.

Request a Demo

Everything you need to manage permits

A complete virtual permitting system designed for the complexity of higher education.

Unlimited Permit Rules & Types

Create any number of permit types with complex eligibility rules, pricing tiers, and validity periods.

Guest/Visitor/Event Management

Streamlined visitor registration, event parking allocation, and guest pass generation in one place.

Automated Communications & Waitlist

Customizable HTML emails, SMS notifications, automatic waitlist management, and renewal reminders.

Secure Integrations (API-first, SSO, 2FA)

Enterprise-grade security with single sign-on, two-factor authentication, and a comprehensive API.

Payroll Deduction Support

Integrate with your HR system for payroll deductions, reducing administrative overhead and improving collections.

Self-Service Portal

Empower students, staff, departments, and contractors to manage their own permits, vehicles, and payments online.

How it works

01

Configure Rules

Define permit types, eligibility criteria, pricing tiers, and zone assignments — no coding required.

02

Enable Self-Service

Launch your branded portal where parkers can register, select permits, add vehicles, and pay.

03

Automate Communications

Set up email and SMS workflows for approvals, renewals, waitlist updates, and policy changes.

04

Monitor & Report

Track utilization, revenue, and compliance through real-time dashboards and automated reports.
