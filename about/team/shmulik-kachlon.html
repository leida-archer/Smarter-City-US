Shmulik “Sam” Kachlon

Chief Technology Officer
Owner & Co-founder

From Code to Cloud since 2012 — Engineering the Future of Urban Mobility.

Connect on LinkedIn

Shmulik Kachlon brings a career-long foundation in software development, security, and technical leadership. Prior to joining SCS, he held senior leadership roles in the biometric and banking industries, building a strong track record in delivering secure, scalable technology solutions.

At SCS, Shmulik has evolved from architect to technical leader, building and mentoring a high-performing team focused on customer-centric product development. His leadership has guided the organization’s transition to modern engineering practices and a fully cloud-native infrastructure powered by AWS.

Combining strategic vision with a hands-on approach, Shmulik has played a key role in reshaping traditional parking management software—enabling rapid innovation and delivering a flexible suite of digital services that continuously adapt to customer needs.
