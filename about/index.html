15+ Years of Parking Innovation

From pioneering mobile parking payments in Australia to modernizing campus parking across the United States.

Our journey

What started as a mobile payments startup in Australia has become the most integrated parking platform in higher education. Our story is one of continuous innovation driven by real customer needs.

2008

CellOPark Australia

Founded as one of Australia's first mobile parking payment companies. Pioneered pay-by-phone technology across major Australian cities.

2010-2017

University Market Leadership

Expanded into higher education, capturing 75% of the Australian and New Zealand university parking market with an integrated platform approach.

2018

Smarter City Solutions Rebrand

Rebranded to reflect our broader vision: not just parking payments, but a complete smart city parking ecosystem.

2024+

US Expansion

Launched in the United States with Harvard, Princeton, Chapman, St. Edward's, and more. Bringing 15+ years of proven technology to American campuses.
