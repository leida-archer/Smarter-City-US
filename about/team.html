The Team Behind SCS

A passionate team of parking technology experts, engineers, and customer success professionals.

Executive Leadership

Ori Almog

Chief Executive Officer
Owner & Co-founder

As co-founder and CEO, Ori has shaped SCS into an award-winning provider of PAYG and vPermit solutions across Australia and New Zealand — with integrated-system work recognized by the International Parking Institute.

Jim Leida

Chief Operating Officer
Owner & US Manager

With 15+ years in parking operations, Jim focuses on the challenges facing higher-education campuses of every size, leading nationwide rollouts of LPR-enabled virtual permitting and enforcement that improve efficiency and compliance.

Shmulik “Sam” Kachlon

Chief Technology Officer
Owner & Co-founder

A career-long technology leader from the biometric and banking sectors, Shmulik has guided SCS to modern engineering and a cloud-native AWS stack, reshaping parking software into a flexible suite of digital services.

Delivery & Support

Mike Adams

Director of Growth
 

With 15+ years across SaaS, PARCS, and complex parking operations, Mike leads growth at SCS — expanding business opportunities and deepening partner relationships with a focus on the customer experience.

Matt Hutchinson

Product Manager
 

Matt has delivered large-scale systems across telecom, government, education, finance, and resources. A former SCS client at Curtin University, he pairs PRINCE2, PMI, and Agile delivery with deep operational insight.

Steve Ezrow

Director of Projects & Support
 

With 13+ years spanning operations and the vendor side, Steve has led 50+ parking implementations — SaaS platforms, access control, cameras, and guidance systems — and now drives project execution, training, and issue resolution at SCS.

Sekouba Sissoko

Solution Support & Delivery Specialist
 

With nearly 10 years supporting higher-education and municipal parking, plus prior healthcare IT, Sekouba excels at database configuration, mobile enforcement-device diagnostics, and LPR troubleshooting — methodical and customer-first.
