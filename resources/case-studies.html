UNIVERSITY OF TOLEDO

ParkUToledo — Citation Hold Buffer Innovation

The Challenge

The University of Toledo needed a citation hold buffer — a system that would delay citation issuance for one hour when a vehicle entered without an active permit, giving drivers time to register. No competitor could deliver this feature.

The Solution

SCS customized a one-hour citation hold buffer within the vCompliance platform. If a vehicle entered without a permit, the system would hold the citation, giving the driver time to register before enforcement action.

"SCS customized a one-hour buffer; if a system entered without a permit, the system holds the citation. No one has confidently told us that a product is out there that can do what SCS can do."
Claire Guo, Executive Director, ParkUToledo
