The challenges you're facing

Campus parking is uniquely complex. Legacy systems can't keep up.

Legacy Client-Server Systems

Aging on-premise software from T2, NuPark, or iParq that requires manual updates, local IT support, and can't scale.

SCS Solution: Cloud-native platform with automatic updates, no on-premise infrastructure required.

Manual Enforcement Processes

Officers checking stickers and hangtags, manually logging violations, and dealing with disputes without evidence.

SCS Solution: vCompliance LPR-powered enforcement with real-time validation and photo evidence.

Fragmented Permits & Payments

Separate systems for permits, meters, citations, and payments that don't share data. Reconciliation nightmares.

SCS Solution: One integrated platform — vPermit + vCompliance + vPark sharing a single data layer.
