City parking challenges

Downtown Congestion

Drivers circling for parking accounts for up to 30% of downtown traffic. Real-time availability data reduces cruising.

Citation Revenue Leakage

Manual chalk-and-check enforcement misses violations and creates disputes. LPR captures every one, with evidence.

Citizen Complaints

Broken meters, confusing signage, and cash-only payment frustrate residents and visitors.

Fragmented Meters

Multiple meter vendors, separate payment systems, and no unified reporting across zones and lots.
